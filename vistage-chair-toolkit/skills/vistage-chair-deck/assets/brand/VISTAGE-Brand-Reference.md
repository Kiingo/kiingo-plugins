# Vistage Brand Reference — for PowerPoint

A quick spec sheet for building an on-brand Vistage deck. Logo files are in this same folder.

## Colors

| Role | Name | HEX | RGB | Use |
|------|------|-----|-----|-----|
| Primary | Vistage Navy | `#003E5E` | 0, 62, 94 | Titles, headers, the wordmark, dark backgrounds |
| Accent | Vistage Gold | `#EDC11C` | 237, 193, 28 | Highlights, underlines, key stats, call-to-action |
| Neutral | White | `#FFFFFF` | 255, 255, 255 | Backgrounds, reverse text on navy |
| Neutral (suggested) | Cool Gray | `#5A6B75` | 90, 107, 117 | Body text, captions, secondary detail |

Navy was sampled directly from the official logo. Gold matches Vistage's published accent ("Gold Tips"). The cool gray is a recommended supporting neutral, not an official spec.

**Slide recipe:** white background + navy headlines + gold as a sparing accent (a rule, a number, an icon) reads as cleanly Vistage. For section dividers, flip it: full navy background with white/reverse logo and a gold accent line.

## Typography

- **Brand typeface:** Sofia Pro (geometric sans, what Vistage uses on the web). It's an Adobe Fonts / paid family, so it likely isn't installed in PowerPoint by default.
- **Close free substitutes:** Montserrat or Poppins (both geometric sans, very close in feel; available free via Google Fonts).
- **Safe system fallback:** Calibri or Arial if you need something already on every machine.

Pairing that works: bold weight for headlines (all-caps for big statements, matching the logo), regular/light weight for body.

## Usage notes

- Keep clear space around the logo (roughly the height of the "V" on all sides).
- Don't recolor, stretch, or add effects to the logo. Navy-on-light or white-on-dark only.
- These are Vistage's trademarked assets — appropriate for a Vistage-affiliated presentation, not for implying endorsement of an unrelated product.
