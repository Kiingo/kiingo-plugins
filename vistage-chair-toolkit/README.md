# Vistage Chair Toolkit

The complete toolkit for peer advisory group facilitators. Design meetings, process issues, generate exercises, plan curricula, coach members one-to-one, onboard new members, manage guest days, drive accountability, curate group composition, build market maps, activate referrals, monitor public triggers, revive stalled prospects, scan prospect signals, research prospects, build outreach campaigns, handle objections, practice sales conversations, and grow your practice — all purpose-built for the unique demands of running CEO peer groups.

Built for Vistage Chairs, EO moderators, YPO forum officers, TEC Chairs, and anyone who facilitates peer advisory groups.

## Components

### Skills — Facilitation

| Skill | What it does |
|-------|-------------|
| **Meeting Architect** | Designs complete meetings with room-undercurrent maps, agenda triage, Chair run-of-show, energy architecture, live pivot scripts, exercises, pre-work, follow-up, decision logs, and accountability systems |
| **Issue Processing** | Upgrades the 5-step issue processing framework with issue triage, advanced clarification protocols, root cause toolkits (5 Whys, Iceberg, Fishbone, Cynefin), solution generation methods, and commitment enforcement |
| **Exercise Lab** | Generates decision-forcing exercises with selection tags for trust level, time, guest-safety, remote use, group size, energy, facilitation risk, full scripts, worksheets, and variations |
| **Curriculum Toolkit** | Designs curriculum as retention and group-health strategy with 12-month red-thread arcs, veteran stretch, renewal-risk planning, check-in diagnostics, speaker integration, and stagnation repair |
| **Humanizer** | Identifies and removes 24 patterns of AI-generated writing to make text sound natural and human |

### Skills — Member Experience & Group Stewardship

| Skill | What it does |
|-------|-------------|
| **One-to-One Coach** | Prepares private monthly member coaching sessions with member pattern diagnosis, renewal-risk radar, veteran Chair intervention scripts, commitment capture, and guidance on whether an issue belongs in the group |
| **Member Onboarding** | Designs first-90-day onboarding with room integration, old-guard resistance handling, confidentiality norms, buddy design, first-value creation, first issue timing, and integration risk detection |
| **Guest Day Converter** | Plans authentic guest visits, preps the guest and group, chooses participation mode, guides post-meeting debriefs, and assesses fit without turning the meeting into a sales performance |
| **Accountability System** | Builds decision logs, commitment quality audits, commitment debt flags, accountability partner design, non-shaming escalation, accountability resets, and quarterly progress reviews |
| **Group Composition** | Analyzes room design, industry conflicts, company size mix, personality mix, member additions/removals, succession inside the group, and second-group readiness |

### Skills — Recruitment & Growth

| Skill | What it does |
|-------|-------------|
| **Market Map Builder** | Helps Chairs start from scratch by defining the room, building a seat thesis, choosing target segments, mapping Chair credibility lanes, building a public-source company universe, scoring fit/timing/access/room value/conflict risk, identifying decision makers, mapping referral sources, and designing warm trust paths |
| **Chair Authority Builder** | Builds reputation gravity with authority thesis, credibility lanes, profile positioning, pattern library, content pillars, LinkedIn/manual content drafts, newsletters, speaking topics, advisor briefings, roundtables, and 30/60/90-day cadence |
| **Referral Source Briefs** | Prepares source-specific briefs for members, alumni, advisors, past guests, and community connectors with likely access, motivation, reputation risk, fit education, ask level, scripts, intro copy, and close-loop plan |
| **Referral Activation Engine** | Turns members, alumni, advisors, past guests, and community connectors into a trust-preserving referral system with source maps, specific asks, intro copy, follow-up cadence, close-loop language, and CRM tracking fields |
| **Public Trigger Monitor** | Builds a public-web monitoring workflow and weekly trigger digest so Chairs can spot expansion, leadership, ownership, award, hiring, succession, and event signals, then decide whether to act, ask for a referral, run a signal scan, nurture, or pass |
| **Stalled Prospect Reviver** | Diagnoses ghosted, deferred, post-guest, "not now," and old "almost joined" prospects; decides revive, nurture, park, or pass; finds a legitimate re-entry reason; drafts relationship-safe manual revival messages; and updates stalled-pipeline CRM fields |
| **Prospect Signals** | Produces a compact public-web recruiting signal fiche: trigger events, durable ownership facts, company developments, leader visibility, Vistage relevance, and room-fit questions without LinkedIn automation |
| **Prospect Intel** | Researches a prospective member — company analysis, leadership profile, communication style, Vistage fit scorecard, room-fit overlay, and tailored conversation strategy with opening moves and discovery questions |
| **Recruitment Playbook** | Strategic recruitment advisor with the Recruitment Flywheel, Referral Engine, Digital Discovery Funnel, weekly Chair growth operating system, and 30/60/90-day recruiting sprints |
| **Outreach Campaign** | Builds a complete multi-touch outreach campaign for a specific prospect with source-specific variants for referrals, events, cold LinkedIn, past guests, stalled prospects, and speaker attendees |
| **Objection Battlecards** | Eight detailed battlecards covering every major objection (time, cost, fit, trust, confidentiality, redundancy), with psychology-backed response frameworks, Chair story banks, and a quick battle guide |
| **Sales Coach** | Interactive conversation coach with CEO roleplay, real-call debriefs, Chair selling-posture diagnosis, fit-protection scoring, objection decoding, and next-step discipline |

### Commands

| Command | What it does |
|---------|-------------|
| `/meeting-prep [theme]` | Quick-start a complete meeting design for your next group session |
| `/exercise [topic]` | Generate three decision-forcing exercises on any business topic |
| `/check-in [context]` | Get curated check-in and icebreaker questions matched to your group |

### Optional Connectors

| Connector | Purpose |
|-----------|---------|
| Email (Outlook, Gmail) | Send pre-work and follow-up emails directly to group members |

## Usage

Install the plugin, then use the slash commands for quick access or just describe what you need:

**Facilitation:**
- "Help me prep for next Tuesday's meeting on pricing"
- "I need an exercise on succession planning for an advanced group"
- "Give me a guest-safe 45-minute exercise for a low-trust group"
- "Give me some deep check-in questions — my group has high trust"
- "Design my 12-month curriculum around leadership and decision-making"
- "Build a curriculum for a stagnant group that needs more accountability"
- "Process an issue: my member is avoiding a people decision"
- "Is this issue ready for the full group, or should I handle it one-to-one?"
- "Humanize this follow-up email I drafted"

**Member Experience & Group Stewardship:**
- "Prep my one-to-one with a member who keeps avoiding people decisions"
- "Create a first-90-day onboarding plan for a new member"
- "I have a prospect visiting as a guest next month — prep the guest and the group"
- "Our commitments are getting vague and follow-through is slipping"
- "Should I add this prospect to my group, or is there an industry conflict?"
- "How do I transition a veteran member who is no longer getting challenged?"

**Recruitment & Growth:**
- "I'm starting from scratch — build my market map"
- "Find the best target segments for a new CEO group in San Diego"
- "Help me build authority with CEOs in my market"
- "Rewrite my LinkedIn profile and create speaking topics for founder-led CEOs"
- "Brief me on this CPA before I ask for referrals"
- "Create source briefs for these five advisors and members"
- "Activate referrals from my members and advisor network for these open seats"
- "Write specific referral asks and intro language for my top 10 sources"
- "Monitor my target companies for public trigger events"
- "Create a weekly trigger digest for my San Diego manufacturing prospects"
- "Revive this prospect who said not now three months ago"
- "Audit my stalled prospect list and tell me who to re-engage"
- "Write a dignified reactivation note after a guest day"
- "Pull a prospect signal fiche for Acme Corp before I reach out"
- "Research John Smith, CEO of Acme Corp — I'm meeting him next week"
- "Add a room-fit overlay for this prospect before I invite them as a guest"
- "Help me fill my group — I have 3 open seats"
- "Build a 90-day recruiting sprint and weekly growth operating system"
- "Build an outreach campaign for the CEO I just researched"
- "Write a referral-based outreach sequence for this prospect"
- "They said they don't have time — how do I handle that?"
- "Build a story bank for cost and time objections"
- "Let's practice a sales call — play a skeptical CEO"
- "I just had a call with a prospect who said she needs to think about it — debrief me"
