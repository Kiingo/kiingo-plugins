# Vistage Chair Toolkit

The complete toolkit for peer advisory group facilitators. Design meetings, process issues, generate exercises, plan curricula, research prospects, build outreach campaigns, handle objections, practice sales conversations, and grow your practice — all purpose-built for the unique demands of running CEO peer groups.

Built for Vistage Chairs, EO moderators, YPO forum officers, TEC Chairs, and anyone who facilitates peer advisory groups.

## Components

### Skills — Facilitation

| Skill | What it does |
|-------|-------------|
| **Meeting Architect** | Designs complete meetings with agendas, energy management, exercises, facilitation scripts, pre-work emails, follow-up emails, decision logs, and accountability systems |
| **Issue Processing** | Upgrades the 5-step issue processing framework with advanced clarification protocols, root cause toolkits (5 Whys, Iceberg, Fishbone, Cynefin), solution generation methods, and commitment enforcement |
| **Exercise Lab** | Generates decision-forcing exercises with full facilitation scripts, worksheets, and variations. Includes a format library with 30+ formats across 8 categories |
| **Curriculum Toolkit** | Designs 12-month strategic curricula, icebreaker question banks, member lifecycle management, group health diagnostics, and Chair practice strategy |
| **Humanizer** | Identifies and removes 24 patterns of AI-generated writing to make text sound natural and human |

### Skills — Recruitment & Growth

| Skill | What it does |
|-------|-------------|
| **Prospect Intel** | Researches a prospective member — company analysis, leadership profile, DISC communication style, Vistage fit scorecard, and tailored conversation strategy with opening moves and discovery questions |
| **Recruitment Playbook** | Strategic recruitment advisor with the Recruitment Flywheel, Referral Engine, Digital Discovery Funnel, and a prioritized 10-step action plan customized to your specific situation |
| **Outreach Campaign** | Builds a complete multi-touch outreach campaign for a specific prospect — emails, LinkedIn messages, value bombs, conversion message, timing calendar, subject lines, and objection responses, all copy-paste ready |
| **Objection Battlecards** | Eight detailed battlecards covering every major objection (time, cost, fit, trust, confidentiality, redundancy), with psychology-backed response frameworks, story arsenals, and a quick battle guide |
| **Sales Coach** | Interactive two-mode coach — Practice Mode runs a realistic roleplay with a simulated CEO prospect and real-time coaching feedback; Debrief Mode analyzes a real conversation and builds a strategic game plan for next steps |

### Commands

| Command | What it does |
|---------|-------------|
| `/meeting-prep [theme]` | Quick-start a complete meeting design for your next group session |
| `/exercise [topic]` | Generate three decision-forcing exercises on any business topic |
| `/check-in [context]` | Get curated check-in and icebreaker questions matched to your group |

### Optional Connectors

| Connector | Purpose |
|-----------|---------|
| Email (Outlook, Gmail) | Send pre-work and follow-up emails directly to group members |

## Usage

Install the plugin, then use the slash commands for quick access or just describe what you need:

**Facilitation:**
- "Help me prep for next Tuesday's meeting on pricing"
- "I need an exercise on succession planning for an advanced group"
- "Give me some deep check-in questions — my group has high trust"
- "Design my 12-month curriculum around leadership and decision-making"
- "Process an issue: my member is avoiding a people decision"
- "Humanize this follow-up email I drafted"

**Recruitment & Growth:**
- "Research John Smith, CEO of Acme Corp — I'm meeting him next week"
- "Help me fill my group — I have 3 open seats"
- "Build an outreach campaign for the CEO I just researched"
- "They said they don't have time — how do I handle that?"
- "Let's practice a sales call — play a skeptical CEO"
- "I just had a call with a prospect who said she needs to think about it — debrief me"

## Who this is for

Peer advisory group facilitators who want to:
- Run meetings that produce real decisions, not just good conversations
- Build 12-month curricula that compound value month over month
- Process member issues at a deeper level than surface-level advice
- Generate fresh exercises so their group never goes on autopilot
- Send polished, human-sounding communications to their members
- Research prospects thoroughly before every conversation
- Build systematic recruitment pipelines instead of hoping for referrals
- Create personalized outreach that gets responses from busy executives
- Handle objections with confidence and psychological insight
- Practice and improve their sales conversations with real-time coaching
