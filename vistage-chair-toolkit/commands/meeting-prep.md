Design a complete meeting for a CEO peer advisory group using the meeting-architect skill.

If a theme was provided via $ARGUMENTS, use it. Otherwise, ask the Chair for:
1. Theme for the meeting
2. Group size and composition (industries, company sizes)
3. Meeting length (full-day or half-day)
4. Group maturity (new, established, or advanced)

Follow the full meeting-architect workflow:
1. Diagnose before designing — assess theme fit, group dynamics, timing
2. Design the meeting architecture with energy management across the day
3. Create 2-3 exercises with complete facilitation scripts
4. Build the materials package: pre-work email, follow-up email template, decision log, accountability partner guide, and facilitation dashboard

If the Chair wants to send the pre-work email, draft it and offer to send via ~~email.

Deliver everything as copy-paste-ready materials the Chair can use in their next meeting.
