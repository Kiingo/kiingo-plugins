Generate a decision-forcing exercise for a CEO peer advisory group using the exercise-lab skill.

If a topic was provided via $ARGUMENTS, use it as the decision category. Otherwise, ask the Chair for:
1. Decision category (pricing, talent, stratecy, cash, succession, AI adoption, etc.)
2. Group maturity (new, established, or advanced)
3. Time available (45, 60, 75, or 90 minutes)
4. Any specific scenario the group is facing

Generate three exercises: one tactical (30-day action), one strategic (90-day directional choice), one developmental (organizational/leadership pattern).

For each exercise, include:
- Memorable name and the specific decision it forces
- Complete facilitation script with exact opening lines
- Phase-by-phase timing and instructions
- Facilitation moves for common challenges (dominant member, quiet member, advice spray, energy collapse)
- One-page worksheet design with a Decision Capture Box
- Variations for different contexts (struggling company, high-growth, virtual, low trust, time-compressed)

Make everything copy-paste ready for the Chair's next meeting.
