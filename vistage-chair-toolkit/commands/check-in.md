Generate check-in or icebreaker questions for a CEO peer advisory group using the curriculum-toolkit and exercise-lab skills.

If context was provided via $ARGUMENTS, use it. Otherwise, ask the Chair for:
1. Group maturity (new, established, or advanced)
2. Today's meeting theme (if any)
3. Current group energy (high, moderate, or low)

Produce 6-8 curated questions organized by depth level:
- 2 light questions (warm-up, low stakes)
- 2-3 reflective questions (building depth)
- 2-3 deep questions (high trust, high candor)

If a theme was provided, include 2 theme-connected questions that prime the brain for the day's work.

For each question, note:
- Which group maturity level it fits
- Suggested time per person (30 or 60 seconds)
- A brief tip on what to listen for as the facilitator

Remind the Chair: go first and model the depth you want.
